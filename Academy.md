# Cobblemon Academy - Player Guide

> Changes from the base modpack for Cobblemon Academy Legacy (~10 players).

---

## Server Goal: Gym Leader (Enfin c'est qu'une idÃ©e ...)

Each player must build their own gym, with a maximum of **2 types**. Once everyone has progressed enough, a **Gym Leader Championship** event will be organized.

### Event Rules

- Each gym will be **judged on its aesthetics**.
- **Battles** will take place between players.
- Each player must prepare **2 teams**:
  1. **Gym Team**: a team matching your gym's type theme.
  2. **Challenger Team**: a **fixed** team used to challenge other players' gyms and earn their badges.

### Spawn Monument

A monument at spawn displays which player is the Gym Leader for which type. Ask an **OP** to update your types.

## Structures & Exploration

Structure density has been increased compared to the default modpack.

### Notable Structures

- **Alpha Dens**: contain stronger Alpha Pokemon.
- **Mega Showdown Sites**: archaeological sites, meteorites, and structures containing **Mega Stones**.
- **Wishing Weald**: required for **Dynamax** (Power Spot, 20-block range).
- **Rad Gyms**: 18 type-themed gyms spread across the world. They contain loot and a chance at legendary items.
- **Eternal Starlight Structures**: cursed gardens, golem forges, portal ruins.
- **Copper Tower, Arenas, Volcanoes, Cathedrals** and more.
- **Vanilla Minecraft**: abandoned mineshafts, buried treasures, and Trial Chambers are active.

## Legendary Items

### How to Obtain Them

Legendary items are found in **world chests**. Drop chance is based on **distance from spawn**:

| Distance from Spawn | Legendary Item Chance |
|----------------------|------------------------|
| Spawn (t0) | None |
| 1,000 blocks and beyond (t1-t9) | **1/50 per chest** |

> On average, about **50 chests** (roughly 3-4 villages) to find one legendary item.

### Gym Loot

Each Rad Gym victory gives a **1/20** chance of a legendary item, regardless of distance from spawn. Gyms also have a **1/15** chance of dropping a **TM/CT**.

### Unique Items

Each legendary item is **unique per server**. Once a player obtains an item, no one else can find it. This encourages trading between players.

### How It Works

1. You find a legendary item in a chest.
2. You receive a placeholder item that transforms into a random legendary item, filtered by the **biome** you're in.
3. The item is consumed when used to summon the corresponding legendary Pokemon.
4. A **server-wide broadcast** announces the spawn to all players.

## Alerts & Broadcasts

The server automatically alerts players when special Pokemon spawn:

- **Shinies**, **Legendaries**, **Mythicals**, **Ultra Beasts**, **Paradox**

Alerts display the **biome** and **nearest player** (no exact coordinates).

When a player summons a legendary or mythical, a **server-wide broadcast** is sent.
